Introduction

The Expense Tracker System is a system that allows you to keep track of your expenses. You can add, view, edit and delete expenses. You can also register and login to the system.

Why track the usage of your expenses?

Tracking your expenses is important because it helps you to know where your money is going. It helps you to identify areas where you can cut back on spending and save money. It also helps you to set financial goals and track your progress towards achieving them.

Why use the system?

The Expense Tracker System makes it easy for you to keep track of your expenses. You can add, view, edit and delete expenses with just a few clicks. You can also register and login to the system to access your expenses from anywhere.

How to use the system?

To use the Expense Tracker System, you need to register and login to the system. Once you are logged in, you can add, view, edit and delete expenses. You can also search for expenses by date or category.
